RESEARCH

Research is an integral part of the academic mission of St. Louis University. Thus, it has recognized the need to prioritize and strengthen its research capacities. The research effort is envisioned to be the centerpiece project of the strategic plan of SLU, the breadth and scope of which will include developmental programs in the Cordillera region and adjoining municipalities as well as developing the research culture and creative thinking among the faculty and students. Cognizant of the importance of research in its role as a top university, SLU has established different research units to fully respond to the community's need to study and solve problems related to economic growth, the environment, regional development and sustainability.
